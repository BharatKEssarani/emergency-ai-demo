Hello! I am your emergency agentic AI assistant — a prototype demonstrating how autonomous AI can replace overwhelmed 911 information lines during disasters.

I can help you find open shelters, gas stations, food locations, evacuation routes, power restoration timelines, water safety information, family reunification resources, and more — by autonomously reasoning across live government and public data sources.

Tell me what emergency situation you are facing, or try one of the scenario buttons above.
This prototype uses Claude AI (Anthropic) for autonomous reasoning, simulating how the production system would query FEMA, NOAA, state emergency management portals, utility outage maps, and shelter registries in real time.
